How to Play GUILTY

🎯 Objective

Find the culprit among 10 suspects using trait clues. You have limited guesses!

🎮 How to Play

  • Each suspect has multiple traits (job, access level, alibi, etc.)
  • Click on a suspect to make a guess
  • After each guess, you'll see color-coded feedback for each trait
  • Use the feedback to narrow down the culprit

🎨 Color Guide

Green = Exact match
Yellow = Close (adjacent trait)
Gray = Wrong

💡 Tips

  • Start with the "Evidence Analysis" at the top - green traits are confirmed accurate!
  • Click ❌ to eliminate suspects you've ruled out
  • Check the Reference (📖) to see all possible trait values
  • In Easy mode, yellow traits show arrows pointing toward the correct value

🏆 Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: 8 guesses, 2 confirmed traits, directional arrows
  • Medium: 6 guesses, 1 confirmed trait
  • Hard: 4 guesses, 1 confirmed trait, less reliable clues

New crimes appear twice daily at midnight and noon EST!
